About




Welcome to the Talkmi community, where we share our experiences and connect with others in a safe environment.

Talkmi finds the right people to talk to for your needs, intelligently matching people with similar health concerns and interests for live anonymous conversations.

Our Mission

Provide the real-time support and advice you need, when you need it.

Our Goal

To create an open, sharing, and compassionate community where members are available 24x7 to support each other and foster positive long-lasting relationships.

Open Philosophy

At Talkmi, you will be able to connect and speak anonymously with others in the community. These conversations are archived and made available for the benefit of everyone in the community to revisit. The purpose is to create communication whereby sharing we help each other and show that we are not alone.

Future Features

As we progress, we plan on providing more real-time support services for the community such as expert talks, moderated support groups, and starting your own support group. Please fill out our survey at http://talkmi.com/featuresurvey.html and let us know what features would be most valuable to you.

Please write to us at feedback@talkmi.com and let us know how we can help you get the support you need.
