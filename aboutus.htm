About us
Welcome to the TalkAboutHealth community
TalkAboutHealth is a real-time support group where you can connect with others who have similar health concerns and interests for live, anonymous conversations.


Our mission
To provide everyone the opportunity to get the health support, advice, and answers they need in real-time.


Our goal
To create an open and compassionate community where members are available to support each other and develop meaningful relationships.


 Our sharing philosophy
TalkAboutHealth believes in sharing our experiences and knowledge. Our purpose is to create a community to help each other and expand our collective knowledge. All conversations are archived and made available for the benefit of everyone to revisit.


What TalkAboutHealth can do for you
- Receive the support you need within minutes.
- Expand your support network and develop new friendships.
- Learn from the experiences and knowledge of the community.


 Anonymous and secure
You will only be identified by your user name during conversations. You determine how much information to share in your profile and whether your profile is viewable or private to the community. Additionally, TalkAboutHealth takes all reasonable precautions to protect your personal information.


Our promise
We respect privacy and will never share any personally identifiable information with anyone without your explicit permission.


Team and advisors
TalkAboutHealth is led by a team and advisors concerned about your health. We have qualified physicians, pharmacists, and therapists on our medical advisory board.


Future features
As we grow, we plan on providing more real-time support services for the community such as expert talks and moderated support groups. If there are additional features you would like to see at TalkAboutHeath or ways that you think we can improve, please fill out our survey at Feature Survey

If you would like to contact us please write to us at feedback@talkabouthealth.com
